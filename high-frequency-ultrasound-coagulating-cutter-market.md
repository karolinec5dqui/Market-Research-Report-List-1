<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Ultrasound Coagulating Cutter market comprises two main types: monopolar and bipolar devices. Monopolar cutters use a single active electrode to generate high-frequency currents, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation in surgical procedures. In contrast, bipolar cutters utilize two electrodes to create a circuit, offering more localized energy delivery and reduced thermal spread. Both types are designed to enhance surgical efficiency, minimize bleeding, and improve tissue preservation during invasive operations in various medical fields.</p></p>
