<p><p>The digital temperature and humidity sensor market is categorized into two main types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals that represent temperature and humidity levels, allowing for straightforward integration with various systems. In contrast, digital output sensors transmit discrete data in a digital format, making them more compatible with modern electronic devices and enhancing precision. Both types serve diverse applications, including HVAC systems, weather stations, and smart home devices, catering to different user needs and technologies.</p></p>
