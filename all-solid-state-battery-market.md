<p><p>The All-Solid-State Battery market can be classified into two primary types: polymer-based and inorganic solid electrolyte batteries. Polymer-based all-solid-state batteries utilize polymer electrolytes, offering flexibility and lightweight properties, making them suitable for various applications, including portable electronics and electric vehicles. In contrast, all-solid-state batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes employ traditional inorganic materials like ceramics, providing higher ionic conductivity and thermal stability, ideal for high-performance applications in electric vehicles and large-scale energy storage systems.</p></p>
