<p><ul><li>Double Row Angular Contact Ball Bearing</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric compressor bearing market is primarily characterized by two types: double row angular contact ball bearings and deep groove ball bearings. Double row angular contact ball bearings provide high load capacity and stability, making them ideal for handling axial and radial loads simultaneously in electric compressors. In contrast, deep groove ball bearings are versatile and suitable for various applications, offering low friction and noise, thus enhancing the efficiency and lifespan of electric compressors. Both types cater to specific performance needs in the industry.</p></p>
