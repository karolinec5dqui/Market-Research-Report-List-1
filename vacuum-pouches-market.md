<p><p>The vacuum pouches market encompasses various film types, each serving specific packaging needs. Polyethylene (PE) offers flexibility and moisture resistance, making it ideal for food packaging. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) provides strength and a clear barrier, enhancing product visibility while protecting contents. Polypropylene (PP) is known for its high temperature resistance and is commonly used for non-food items. Nylon (NY) adds durability and gas barrier properties, suitable for sensitive products. Aluminum (AL) pouches offer excellent light and moisture barriers, extending product shelf life.</p></p>
