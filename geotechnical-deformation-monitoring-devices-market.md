<p><p>The geotechnical deformation monitoring devices market encompasses two primary types: geodetic measuring devices and geotechnical measuring devices. Geodetic measuring devices utilize satellite and terrestrial methods for monitoring large-scale land deformations, providing precise spatial data. In contrast, geotechnical measuring devices focus on assessing soil and rock stability, employing sensors to detect changes in subsurface conditions. Together, these technologies enable comprehensive monitoring of structural integrity and earth movements, essential for construction, mining, and geological hazard management.</p></p>
