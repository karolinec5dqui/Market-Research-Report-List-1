<p><p>The multimetal dielectric nanocomposites market is categorized into high-k and low-k types based on their dielectric constant. High-k multimetal dielectric nanocomposites possess a high dielectric constant, enhancing capacitance and allowing for miniaturized electronic devices without compromising performance. In contrast, low-k multimetal dielectric nanocomposites have a lower dielectric constant, reducing capacitance and minimizing signal delay, making them suitable for high-speed electronic applications. Both types are essential in advancing semiconductor technology and improving performance in electronic components.</p></p>
