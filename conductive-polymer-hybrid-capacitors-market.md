<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Radial Lead Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive polymer hybrid capacitors market is categorized mainly into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type and Radial Lead Type. SMD type capacitors are designed for surface mounting on circuit boards, allowing for compact and efficient layouts, ideal for modern electronics. In contrast, Radial Lead Type capacitors feature leads extending from the cylindrical body, facilitating easy integration into through-hole applications. Both types offer advantages such as enhanced performance, size adaptability, and improved electrical characteristics, catering to diverse consumer electronics and industrial applications.</p></p>
