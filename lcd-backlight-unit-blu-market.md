<p><strong>The LCD Backlight Unit BLU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market is primarily segmented into two types: edge type and direct type. The edge type utilizes light-emitting diodes (LEDs) positioned along the screen's periphery, providing a slim profile and energy efficiency, making it suitable for thinner displays. In contrast, the direct type has LEDs placed directly behind the screen, offering uniform brightness and improved color accuracy, ideal for high-quality visuals in larger screens. Each type serves different applications and consumer preferences in the display industry.</p></p>
