<p><p>The construction insulation materials market comprises various types, each offering distinct thermal and acoustic properties. Glass wool is lightweight and fire-resistant, ideal for walls and roofs. Rockwool, or stone wool, provides excellent fire resistance and soundproofing. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is cost-effective and widely used in thermal insulation. Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) offers superior moisture resistance and durability. Polyurethane foams provide high insulation value, while polyisocyanurate has enhanced thermal performance. Phenolic foams are known for low thermal conductivity, ensuring efficient energy usage. Other materials include cellulose and cotton insulation.</p></p>
