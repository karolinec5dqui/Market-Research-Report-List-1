<p><ul><li>Half Wave</li><li>Full Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rectifier controllers are essential components in converting AC to DC power in various applications. The market can be divided into two main types: half-wave and full-wave rectifiers. Half-wave rectifiers utilize only one half of the input waveform, resulting in lower efficiency and higher ripple voltage. In contrast, full-wave rectifiers use both halves of the waveform, providing better efficiency, reduced ripple, and improved performance. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements and output characteristics needed for optimal performance.</p></p>
